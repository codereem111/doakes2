How to Buy

It's easy to join the watch. Just follow these simple steps to acquire your $DOAKES.

  1. Step 1: Get a Phantom Wallet

    First, you'll need a Phantom wallet. You can download the browser extension or mobile app from the official Phantom website. It's the most secure way to interact with the Solana blockchain.

  2. Step 2: Fund Your Wallet with SOL

    You can buy Solana (SOL) directly on Phantom, or send SOL from a centralized exchange like Coinbase or Binance to your Phantom wallet address. You'll need SOL to buy $DOAKES and cover transaction fees.

  3. Step 3: Head to a DEX

    Connect your Phantom wallet to a decentralized exchange (DEX) on the Solana network, such as Raydium or Jupiter. Look for the "Swap" or "Trade" section.

  4. Step 4: Swap for $DOAKES

    In the DEX, select SOL as your "from" currency and enter the $DOAKES contract address in the "to" currency field. Enter the amount of SOL you wish to swap, confirm the transaction, and you're officially a part of the watch!
